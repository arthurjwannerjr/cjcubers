Putting Our Experience to Work for You

At CJ Cubers, we've been involved in alfalfa production since the 70's. We became involved in the exporting of alfalfa hay cubes in 1988. In 1995, we added bale compressing capabilities at our stationary cubing facility and began offering compressed baled hay in conjunction with cubes. In 2010 the CJ Cubers production facilities were sold to Sage Hill Northwest, where they continue the tradition of exporting the finest hay products available. We will still be working closely with Sage Hill to supply products to specific markets. CJ Cubers continues on as a 3-tie bailing operation, helping farmers around the Columbia Basin put up their hay efficiently and professionally.
