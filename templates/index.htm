Putting Our Experience to Work for You

At CJ Cubers, we've been involved in alfalfa production since the 70's. We became involved in the production and exporting of alfalfa hay cubes in 1988. In 1995, we added bale compressing capabilities at our stationary cubing facility and began offering compressed baled hay in conjunction with cubes. Our mission is to continue to provide the best possible livestock feed at the best price to ensure your ultimate satisfaction. Whatever you need, you're sure to find at CJ Cubers.
